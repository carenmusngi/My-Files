Beginner

Give yourself plenty of time to warm up and cool down with easy walking or gentle stretching. Then speed up to a pace you can continue for five to 10 minutes without getting overly tired. As your stamina improves, gradually increase the amount of time you exercise.

Intermediate

If you've been working out consistently for the last six months or more, then you're most likely at an intermediate level. At this point, you've already completed a beginner program that allowed you to build up a base level of strength and endurance.

Advanced

Advanced trainees use advanced exercises that challenge their bodies for increased strength, power, speed, and extra intensity to achieve added results. They have years of consistent experience working their body and know how to manipulate variables to challenge their training needs.
